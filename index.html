About SwasthSetu

SwasthSetu is a crowdsourced platform where volunteers help transport patients to medical centers, ensuring better healthcare access.

Key Features

Volunteer Transport

Volunteers offer non-emergency rides, earning rewards for their service.

Live Ambulance Tracking

Real-time tracking for ambulances, ensuring faster emergency response.

Women-Specific Support

Option to request female volunteers for added safety and comfort.

Multi-Language Support

Inclusive features for different communities, including text-to-speech.
